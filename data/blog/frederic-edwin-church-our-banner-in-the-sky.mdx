* Title: Our banner in the sky
* Artist: Frederic Edwin Church (1826-1900)
* Date: 1861
* Medium: Lithography
* Dimensions: 19 x 29 cm
* Location: Private collection

photograph of a lithograph by Frederick Edwin Church the subject of which was created in 1861 as an oil painting by Church - proceeds from sales of the lithograph were dedicated to the families of Union troops - the colorful sunset inspired the renown artist when he interpreted the resemblance of the sunset to the Union flag as implying divine support for the Union to prevail in the American Civil War
