* Title: Saint Mark from Garima gospels
* Artist:  Anonymous (-)
* Date: c.500
* Medium: Ink and tempera on vellum
* Dimensions: 

The Garima Gospels are two ancient Gospel Books believed to be the earliest surviving complete illuminated Christian manuscript. They are preserved in an isolated monastery in the Ethopian Tigray region set among 2000 meter-high mountains.
