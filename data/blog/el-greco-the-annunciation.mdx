* Title: The Annunciation
* Artist:  El Greco (1541-1614)
* Date: c.1596-1600
* Medium: Oil on canvas
* Dimensions: 113 x 65 cm
* Location: ?

El Greco was a big admirer of Corregio and also of Pamiggiano and both artists' work can be traced as influence in El Greco's Annunciation- the light/dark of Corregio (the  dove representing the Holy Spirit is the source of light) and the elongated figures and sharp colors of Parmiggiano.
