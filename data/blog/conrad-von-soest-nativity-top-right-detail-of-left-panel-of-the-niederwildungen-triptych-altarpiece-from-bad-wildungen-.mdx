* Title: Nativity (top-right detail of left panel of the Niederwildungen triptych altarpiece from Bad Wildungen)
* Artist: Conrad von Soest (c.1370-c.1422)
* Date: 1403
* Medium: Oil on wood
* Dimensions: 74 x 61 cm

This scene of the “Nativity” is very much a precursor of scène de genre mixing religious iconography and everyday life of the 15th century in Germany.
