* Title: Mars, Venus and Amor
* Artist:  Titian (c.1489-1576)
* Date: c. 1550
* Medium: Oil on canvas
* Dimensions: 97 x 109 cm

The union of Venus, the goddess of love, with Mars, the god of war, would result in a girl, Harmonia, a symbol of life sustaining togetherness against opposing forces.
