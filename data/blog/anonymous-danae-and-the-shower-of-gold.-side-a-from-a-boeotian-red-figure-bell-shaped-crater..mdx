* Title: Danaë and the shower of gold. Side A from a Boeotian red-figure bell-shaped crater.
* Artist:  Anonymous (?-?)
* Date: c. 450-425 BC
* Medium: Paint on terraccotta
* Dimensions:  Height 23 cm x diameter 23 cm
* Location: Le Louvre, Paris, France

The Argive princess Danae reclining on a couch is impregnated by Zeus in the guise of a golden shower.\nDanaë reclines on a bed draped with a patterned sheet. Her hair is arranged up on the back of her head and covered with a net or cloth. She wears a cloth drape open in front exposing her abdomen. Zeus in the form of rain falls on her abdomen.
