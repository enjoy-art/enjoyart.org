* Title: Madonna Enthroned (Maestà)
* Artist:  Giotto (c.1267-1337)
* Date: 1310
* Medium: Tempera and gold on wood panel
* Dimensions: 325 x 204 cm
* Location: Uffizi, Firenze, Italy

Giotto's painting marks a breakthrough due to its newfound naturalism and escape from some constraints of Byzantine art. In many ways it is still Byzantine- flat gold background, icon-like representation, symmetry, stiffness, hierarchy of scale of the figures. Yet the evolution is notable- figures are more realistic and three-dimensional; there is an effort towards perspective; there are more details and finesse in the execution; Mary's cloak is open, her top is revealing her female body, her hair is showing...
