* Title: Ustyug Annunciation (Благовещение Устюжское)
* Artist:  Anonymous (-)
* Date: c. 1120
* Medium: Tempera on wood panel
* Dimensions: 238 × 168 cm
* Location: Tretyakov Gallery, Moscow, Russia

The making of icons started in Ancient Rus' when it converted to Orthodox Christianity in 988 CE and was in the Byzantine art style before evolving. \"Annunciation of Ustyug\" is one of the few Russian icons which survived the Mongol invasion of Rus' in the 13th century. The Virgin Mary already carries Jesus in her womb.
