* Title: Judith Beheading Holofernes (Giuditta e Oloferne)
* Artist:  Caravaggio (1571-1610)
* Date: c.1599-1602
* Medium: Oil on canvas
* Dimensions: 145 x 195 cm
* Location: Galleria Nazionale d'Arte Antica, Rome, Italy

The emotional intensity of Caravaggio's painting is great- Judith looks innocent and repulsed by what she is doing, yet she is going through her definitive business.
