* Title: Woman with a mirror
* Artist:  Titian (c.1489-1576)
* Date: 1515
* Medium: Oil on canvas
* Dimensions: 99 x 76 cm
* Location: Le Louvre, Paris, France

A beautiful woman overlooks a table or parapet on which is a bottle of perfume she might be dipping a finger of her left hand in. With her right hand, she caresses her hair. A man behind her holds a mirror in which the woman gazes at herself while she dresses, and the man lifts another mirror behind her so she can also see herself from behind. \n\nThe painting demonstrates great harmony and also brightness in his use of colors. Here he exalts the woman's beauty and sensuality with the loose hair, the pale and soft skin contrasting with the red lips, the bottle of ointment, the play of the mirrors, the nakedness of the upper arm from the wide opening of her loose shirt...
