* Title: Triumph of Chastity (Trionfo della castità)
* Artist: Lorenzo Lotto (c.1480-1557)
* Date: c.1530
* Medium: Oil on canvas
* Dimensions: 73 x 114 cm

The painting "Triumph of Chastity" depicts an allegorical scene celebrating the virtue of chastity. In the painting, a woman dressed in heavy and modest clothing, representing Chastity, pushing away and driving away fully naked Cupid and Venus.
