* Title: Christ Arrest from the Book of Kells
* Artist:  Anonymous (-)
* Date: 800
* Medium: Ink and tempera on vellum
* Dimensions: 
* Location: Trinity College Library, Dublin, Ireland

The illustrations and ornamentation of the Book of Kells are remarkable in extravagance and complexity combining traditional Christian iconography and symbols with Celtic-influenced figures of humans, animals and mythical beasts, Celtic knots, and interlacing patterns in vibrant colors.
