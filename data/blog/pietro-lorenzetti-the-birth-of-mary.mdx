* Title: The birth of Mary (Natività della Vergine)
* Artist: Pietro Lorenzetti (c.1285-1348)
* Date: 1342
* Medium: Tempera and gold on wood panel
* Dimensions: 187 × 182 cm
* Location: Museo dell'Opera del Duomo, Siena, Italy

The \"Birth of Mary\" is the first known one-point perspective painting. Another novelty- despite columns in the middle, the painting is a whole, in a continuous space. There is sense of everyday life- the style of the clothes worn, objects of the time, Jesus being washed like any new born.
