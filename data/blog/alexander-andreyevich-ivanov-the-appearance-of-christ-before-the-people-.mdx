* Title: The appearance of Christ before the people 
* Artist: Alexander Andreyevich Ivanov (1806-1858)
* Date: c. 1837-57
* Medium: Oil on canvas
* Dimensions: 540 x 750 cm
* Location: Tretyakov Gallery, Moscow

It took 20 years for the artist to complete his work that he treated as a historical representation of the New Testament. Symbolically it also represents poverty in Russia and possibly the hesistancy some can have in following a \"Jesus\".
